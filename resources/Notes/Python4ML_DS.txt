Python for Machine Learning & Data Science Masterclass

The path you'll follow to solve a data problem will lead you to build a data product (report, app, visualization, . . .) or perform a data analysis and present your findings. These are the steps you will follow.
1. Collect and store data- Collect from physical sensors, simulations, surveys, usage data,. . . Then store in Database, CVS files, Excel files, cloud storage. Both collect and store data are Data engineering.

2. Clean and organize data - reorganize, deal with missing data, restructure data. May be done with the previous one.

3. Exploratory data analysis - Data Analysis - perform statistical analysis, visualizations, explore populations. If you are trying to answer a question, you might stop here, building visualizations, write a report, prepare communication material . . .

4. Machine learning models - supervised (to predict future outcomes)or not supervised (to discover patterns in the data). Then use the model to create a service, a dashboard or an application (a data product).
